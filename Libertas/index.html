Libertas is a decentralized audio and podcast streaming service built on the Ethereum Blockchain.

WHAT IS LIBERTAS

Libertas is a decentralised public blockchain and cryptocurrency project. Using blockchain technology content is distributed amongst many nodes in the network instead of a single source such as YouTube or Facebook - Libertas is politically unbiased, censorship resistant and immutable.

We are developing a smart contract platform which seeks to provide the world's first decentralizded audio and podcast streaming service.

Content cannot be removed or deplatformed. Trustless ownership provides content creators and node providers with rewards.

LIBERTAS AT A GLANCE

IMMUTABLE

By using Blockchain technology all content uploaded to the Libertas network is immutable and cannot be taken down.

DECENTRALIZED

The Libertas network will be running on top of the Ethereum network and use distributed storage to achieve full decentralization.

CONTENT REWARDS

Content Creators receive Libertas Tokens directly or indirectly when a user listens to a stream.

TRANSPARENCY

Transactions are visible on the blockchain so users can ensure their Tokens have gone straight to their favourite creator's wallet.

COMMUNITY OWNED

The Libertas Network will be owned by the community as opposed to a single corporate entitity.

LIBERTAS PROTOCOL

Libertas will create a custom protocol that can talk directly to the blockchain to get around internet layer control.

WHAT LIBERTAS STANDS FOR

Our mission at Libertas is to ensure freedom of speech is protected. We believe that content belongs to the content creator and not the platform provider. Creators should be free to speak their mind without fear of being demonetized or deplatformed. Libertas is politcally unbiased and does not believe that big tech censorship, cancel culture or deplatforming contribute towards a healthy debate or advancement of our society.
