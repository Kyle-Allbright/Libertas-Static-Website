HOW IT WORKS

CREATE

Create your podcast or audio broadcast using your usual editing software.

UPLOAD

Upload your Podcast or audio broadcast to the Libertas network.

GET PAID

Content Creators will get rewarded in Libertas Token per confirmed broadcast.

DISTRIBUTED

All content uploaded, automatically gets distributed amongst the entire Libertas Network.

TOKEN ACCESS

Tokens act as an access method to the streams. The content creator keeps your token.

SECURE

Streams can no longer be attacked by Denial of Service (DDOS) attacks or be removed by private companies.

CENTRALIZED VS LIBERTAS

CENTRALIZED LIBERTAS
Immutable All centralized solutions can restrict, block, demonetize or delete your content without warning. Libertas cannot delete, demonetize content once it is on the blockchain.

Censorship and monitoring Censorship and monitoring from Big Tech is reaching Orwellian levels. Libertas censors nobody, and monitors nothing.
