FREEDOM OF SPEECH IS UNDER ATTACK

and Libertas provides an immutable solution.


Here is a small list of online personalities who have either been deplatformed or are on the warning list.

JOE ROGAN

After multiple erroneous articles by CNN referring to Joe Rogan as a 'transphobe' for voicing his support for candiate Bernie Sanders, his videos have often been demonetized by YouTube based on his views, or his guests. In May 2020 Rogan signed an exclusive deal with Spotify that in the near future will see him move his content over to the new platform to escape YouTube's control.

ALEX JONES

Infowars and the Alex Jones Show were the first to be deplatformed by Apple, Google and Facebook simultaneously. Twitter followed soon after deplatforming Alex Jones, thus limiting his ability to speak his mind.

JIMMY FALLON

The American comedian and famed TV host is currently under fire for 'blackfacing' in 1990. He has since apologised and is currently still allowed to continue with the Tonight Show, however the backlash continues.

DR JORDAN PETERSON

Dr Jordon Peterson deleted his Patreon account due to Patreon altering their terms of service regarding political speech. He is currently working on a backup platform to Patreon with Dave Rubin.

DAVID ICKE

In 2020 YouTube removed David Icke's channel due him disputing the science surrounding COVID-19. Mr. Icke did not agree with the WHO or the NHS UK and consequently was deplatformed.

OWEN SHROYER

Owen Shroyer's 'The War Room' channel has been permanently banned from YouTube twice.

CARPE DONKTUM

In 2020 Twitter permanently banned Carpe Donktum (real name Logan Cook) from the platform for repeated Copyright violations limiting his freedom of speech and expression.

DAVE RUBIN

Dave has had multiple warnings and demeonetizations due to his political content. At the beginning of his show he has recently began saying that he hopes it "is not his last".

Whether or not we agree with people's opinions we can all agree on one thing:

Freedom of speech / expression is a fundamental human right.
